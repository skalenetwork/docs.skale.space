### Buy CREDITs on Mainnet

On SKALE Base Mainnet, compute is paid for using **CREDIT tokens**. When you buy credits, they are deposited into your wallet on SKALE Base and are used to cover the compute cost of every transaction.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Mainnet">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check or Buy CREDITS">
        Go to the [CREDITS portal](https://base.skalenodes.com/credits) to check your balance.

        <img
          src="../../assets/skale-base-flows/chain_credits_initial.png"
          alt="CREDITS Menu"
          width="500"
        />

        Select **Buy credits**, choose a payment method — **USDC** or **SKL** — and complete the purchase.

        <img
          src="../../assets/skale-base-flows/chain_credits_purchase.png"
          alt="Buy CREDITS"
          width="400"
        />
      </Step>
      <Step title="Use CREDITS">

        Once purchased, credits are available in your wallet on SKALE Base. Every transaction you send deducts compute from your credit balance — no separate gas token is needed.

        Make sure you have enough CREDITS to deploy contracts and perform the necessary app-specific transactions.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>
      If you run into any issues, reach out to the [SKALE Team on Discord](https://discord.gg/skale).
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Testnet">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Get Testnet CREDITS">

        Head to the [SKALE Base Sepolia faucet](https://base-sepolia-faucet.skale.space) to get free compute CREDITS for testing.

        If you need a larger allocation for extensive dApp testing, open a ticket on the [SKALE Discord](https://discord.gg/skale) and request it from the team.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start Building">

        Testnet CREDITS work the same way as mainnet — they cover the compute cost of transactions. No real payment required.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
