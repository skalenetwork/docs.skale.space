## Overview

SKALE Chains on Ethereum use sFUEL — a gas token with no market value — to achieve zero gas fees for users. sFUEL preserves EVM compatibility and spam protection without forcing users to buy tokens. sChain owners pay predictable monthly costs instead of passing gas fees to end users.

<Note>
Zero gas fees is different from gasless transactions. Gasless relies on proof-of-work magic numbers; zero gas fees uses valueless sFUEL with built-in rate limiting. Both are available on SKALE Ethereum chains.
</Note>

## SKALE Fuel (sFUEL)

sFUEL is minted per chain, cannot be traded, and is distributed by chain operators, dApps, or a [public faucet](https://sfuelstation.com). It behaves like ETH for gas accounting, but with no economic value — so onboarding is instant:

- **For developers:** Deploy immediately; larger contracts and more onchain logic no longer carry gas sticker shock.
- **For users:** Apps can invisibly fund wallets with sFUEL during onboarding, so people transact without thinking about gas.

**Who pays:** sChain Owner

## Benefits

- **No paywall** – Remove the "buy gas first" step from onboarding.
- **Predictable costs** – Chain owners pay monthly, not per transaction.
- **EVM tooling ready** – Works with wallets, SDKs, and libraries as-is.
- **Built-in safety** – Rate limits and optional gas settings prevent abuse.
