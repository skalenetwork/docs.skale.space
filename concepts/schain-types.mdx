SKALE Chains are elastic L1s that can be tuned for permissionless apps, ecosystem hubs, or private/enterprise workloads. They inherit zero gas fees, instant finality, and pooled security while letting operators choose custom policies, access controls, and pricing models.

When you launch a chain, you get configuration rights that can be delegated to teammates, secured with multisig, or fully renounced for community control.
