### Chain Notes

- When bridging ETH from Base Sepolia to the SKALE Chain, ETH is locked as native ETH (msg.value) and minted as an ERC-20 called ETHC on the SKALE Chain
- WETH and ETHC are both ERC-20 on the SKALE Chain but are deposited differently
- This testnet chain is 100% permissionless
- CREATE2Factory, SingletonFactory, Multicall3, and Permit2 are deployed to their canonical addresses

### Testnet Tokens

<Note>
**Feel free to create your own testnet tokens** on the BITE V2 Sandbox for your hackathon project. Use any standard token factory or deploy directly using Remix, Hardhat, or Foundry.
</Note>

Common testnet tokens can be deployed or bridged as needed. For token creation, refer to the [Deploy ERC20 Token](/cookbook/smart-contracts/deploy-erc20-token) guide.

### Chain Notes

- BITE V2 Sandbox is optimized for testing encrypted transaction flows
- Both BITE V1 and V2 encryption protocols are supported
- Ideal for projects exploring private agent communications and encrypted payment flows
