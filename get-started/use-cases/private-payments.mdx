SKALE is built for payments. Privacy, instant finality, zero gas fees, and predictable costs let you scale to billions of transactions.

## Features

**Privacy** - Isolated execution environment with access control.

**Zero gas fees** - Users pay without transaction costs.

**Instant finality** - Payments confirm immediately.

**Flat cost structure** - Predictable monthly fee regardless of activity.

**High throughput** - Process thousands of transactions per second.

## Use Cases

- Micropayments
- Real-time payments
- Cross-border payments
- Subscription services
- Agent-to-agent payments
