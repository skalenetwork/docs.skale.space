SKALE supports autonomous agents at scale. Privacy, zero gas fees, instant finality, and high throughput let agents operate without the constraints of traditional blockchains.

## Why SKALE for Agents

**Privacy** - Isolated execution environment with access control.

**Zero gas fees** - Agents can perform millions of operations without transaction costs.

**Instant finality** - Agents get immediate state confirmation for decision-making.

**High throughput** - Process thousands of transactions per second.

**Messaging layer** - Direct agent-to-agent communication.

## Use Cases

- Trading bots
- AI-powered smart contract interactions
- IoT devices
- Automated DeFi strategies
- Gaming NPCs
- Commerce and discovery agents
