SKALE is built for gaming. Privacy, zero gas fees, instant finality, and native random number generation remove the friction of traditional blockchains.

## Features

**Privacy** - Isolated execution environment with access control.

**Zero gas fees** - Players interact without transaction costs.

**Instant finality** - In-game actions confirm immediately.

**Random number generation** - Built-in verifiable randomness for loot, matchmaking, and more.

**High throughput** - Support thousands of concurrent players.

**Predictable costs** - Flat monthly fee regardless of activity.

## Use Cases

- Web3 games with NFTs and tokens
- Play-to-earn mechanics
- Game asset marketplaces
- On-chain tournaments
- Cross-game assets
- Autonomous NPCs
