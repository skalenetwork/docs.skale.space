Deploy your own blockchain with complete control over configuration, access, and resources. Build private chains for enterprise, public chains for apps, or specialized chains for specific needs.

## Features

**Full customization** - Configure chain size, access control, gas models, and consensus parameters.

**Dedicated resources** - Your chain runs on dedicated validator nodes with consistent performance.

**Privacy** - Isolated execution environment with access control and compliance capabilities.

**Bridges** - Connect to Ethereum and other blockchains.

## Use Cases

- Application-specific chains
- Enterprise blockchains
- Gaming chains
- DeFi chains
- Consortium chains
- Test and development environments
