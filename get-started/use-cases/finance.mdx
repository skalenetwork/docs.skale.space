## Overview

SKALE enables financial applications that require confidentiality, compliance, and high performance. With BITE protocol support for confidential applications and siloed compute environments, SKALE is ideal for financial services that need to meet regulatory requirements while maintaining blockchain benefits.

## Key Features for Finance

### Confidential Transactions

SKALE's BITE protocol enables confidential financial transactions that protect sensitive data while maintaining auditability and compliance.

### Regulatory Compliance

Siloed compute environments enable financial institutions to meet regulatory requirements while leveraging blockchain technology.

### High Performance

SKALE can process thousands of transactions per second with instant finality, critical for financial applications that require real-time settlement.

### Cross-Chain Interoperability

SKALE's bridge infrastructure enables financial applications to interact with multiple blockchain networks and traditional financial systems.

## Use Cases

- **Confidential Payments**: Private payment systems with regulatory compliance
- **Trade Finance**: Supply chain finance with confidential transaction details
- **Asset Management**: Portfolio management with privacy-preserving analytics
- **Lending & Borrowing**: DeFi lending platforms with confidential credit scoring
- **Insurance**: Insurance products with confidential claim processing
- **Compliance & Reporting**: Regulatory reporting with privacy-preserving data aggregation

## BITE Protocol for Finance

The BITE protocol enables:

- **Confidential Financial Data**: Process sensitive financial data securely
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Meet compliance requirements while maintaining privacy
- **Cross-Chain Finance**: Coordinate financial operations across multiple networks
- **Audit Trails**: Maintain auditability while preserving confidentiality
