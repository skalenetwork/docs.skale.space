## DeFi

Privacy, zero gas fees, instant finality, and high throughput for DEXs, lending, yield farming, derivatives, stablecoins, and liquidity pools.

## DePIN

Microtransactions and high throughput for compute networks, storage networks, bandwidth sharing, IoT devices, sensor networks, and energy trading.

## Social

Privacy, zero gas fees, and instant finality for social networks, content creation, social tokens, NFT marketplaces, decentralized identity, and community governance.

## Supply Chain

Traceability, privacy, and interoperability for product tracking, food safety, pharmaceutical tracking, logistics, certification, and counterfeit prevention.

## Healthcare

Privacy and compliance for medical records, clinical trials, drug traceability, telemedicine, health insurance, and research data sharing.

## Identity and Credentials

Privacy and verifiability for digital identity, educational credentials, professional certifications, KYC/AML, access control, and reputation systems.

## Real Estate

Tokenization and transparency for property tokenization, property records, rental platforms, real estate investment, property verification, and automated transactions.
