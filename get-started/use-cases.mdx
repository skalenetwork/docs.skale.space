## Overview

SKALE Network enables a wide range of applications across multiple industries. With zero gas fees, instant finality, high throughput, and support for confidential computing, SKALE is ideal for applications that require scalability, cost-effectiveness, and innovative features.

## Payments

SKALE is ideal for building payment systems that require high throughput, instant finality, and predictable costs. With zero gas fees and instant transaction finality, SKALE enables payment applications that can scale to billions of transactions.

### Key Features for Payments

- **Instant Finality**: Transactions are confirmed immediately without waiting for multiple block confirmations
- **Flat Cost Structure**: Predictable monthly fees instead of fluctuating gas costs
- **High Throughput**: Process thousands of transactions per second

### Use Cases

- **Micropayments**: Zero gas fees enable economically viable micropayments
- **Real-time Payments**: Instant finality enables real-time payment processing
- **Cross-border Payments**: Fast, low-cost international payments
- **Subscription Services**: Predictable costs enable subscription-based payment models
- **Agent-to-Agent Payments**: Autonomous agents can make payments without gas cost concerns

> Learn more: [Payments on SKALE](/developers/payments-on-skale), [Zero Gas Fees](/concepts/gas-fees/zero-gas-fees), [Instant Finality](/concepts/instant-finality)

## Agents

SKALE is purpose-built to support billions of autonomous agents. With zero gas fees, instant finality, and high throughput, agents can operate efficiently at scale without the constraints of traditional blockchain networks.

### Why SKALE for Agents?

- **Zero Gas Fees**: Agents can perform unlimited operations without economic barriers
- **Instant Finality**: Immediate state confirmation for agent decision-making
- **High Throughput**: Support for millions of concurrent agents
- **Agent-to-Agent Communication**: Direct communication between agents via SKALE's messaging layer

### Use Cases

- **Autonomous Trading Agents**: Trading bots that can execute millions of trades without gas cost concerns
- **AI Agents**: AI-powered agents that interact with smart contracts and other agents
- **IoT Agents**: Internet of Things devices that operate autonomously on the blockchain
- **DeFi Agents**: Automated DeFi strategies that can operate at scale
- **Gaming Agents**: NPCs and autonomous game entities that interact with blockchain-based games

## Enterprise & Government

SKALE enables enterprises and government organizations to deploy private, siloed compute environments while maintaining connection to public blockchains. This architecture provides the security and compliance requirements of private infrastructure with the interoperability of public blockchains.

### Key Features

- **Siloed Compute**: Isolated environments perfect for data privacy and regulatory compliance
- **Connected to Public Blockchains**: Bridge assets and messages to and from public blockchains
- **BITE Protocol**: Secure cross-chain communication and trustless execution
- **Compliance**: Built-in features for regulatory compliance
- **Scalability**: Scale to handle enterprise-level transaction volumes

### Use Cases

- **Regulated Industries**: Financial services, healthcare, and other regulated industries can deploy compliant blockchain solutions
- **Government Services**: Public sector applications requiring data sovereignty and compliance
- **Supply Chain**: Private supply chain tracking with public verification capabilities
- **Identity Management**: Private identity systems with selective public verification
- **Confidential Transactions**: Financial transactions requiring privacy and compliance
