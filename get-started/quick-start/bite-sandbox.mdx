## Introduction

Autonomous agents can now discover each other, communicate, and move value — **for real**.

This guide covers getting started with Programmable Privacy features on SKALE. Encrypted Transactions and Conditional Transactions are available on **SKALE Base** (mainnet) and **SKALE Base Sepolia** (testnet). Re-encryption and Confidential Tokens are in Beta on **SKALE Base Sepolia**.

## FAQ & Important Developer Information

**Q: Why is my contract failing to deploy/Out of Gas?**

Solidity contracts should be compiled and deployed with EVM version **Istanbul** or lower on privacy-enabled SKALE chains.

**Q: Need to add SKALE to your Wallet?**

Go to [base-sepolia.skalenodes.com](https://base-sepolia.skalenodes.com/chains/base-testnet) for SKALE Base Sepolia or [base.skalenodes.com](https://base.skalenodes.com/chains/base) for SKALE Base Mainnet. Connect your wallet and click "Connect to Chain" to automatically add the chain to your wallet.
