## Requirements
1. A **[Gnosis SAFE](https://safe.global/)** address on the Ethereum Mainnet, dedicated to SKALE Chain Ownership
2. A **cold wallet** address from a member of the ownership or management group who is technical enough to utilize a cold wallet
3. Send 0.01 ETH to your Gnosis SAFE address
4. Determine the [configurations](/run-a-skale-chain/customize-schain) for how you want to customize your SKALE Chain
5. Reach out to the SKALE Team in [Discord](https://discord.gg/skale) for support creating your sChain
