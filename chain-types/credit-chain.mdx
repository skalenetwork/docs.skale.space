## What Is a Credit Chain?

A credit chain is a SKALE Chain where compute resources are prepaid through **compute credits**. The chain owner purchases credits in a simple subscription, and end users transact with **zero gas fees** — they never need to hold or spend tokens.

This is the recommended model for most applications: predictable costs for you, friction-free experience for your users.

## How It Works

```
Chain owner buys credits (monthly subscription)
  → Credits are deposited into the chain's credit pool
    → Users send transactions freely
      → Compute is deducted from the pool, not from users
```

## Buying Credits

Credits are purchased on the [SKALE Portal](https://portal.skale.space/chains):

1. Go to **https://portal.skale.space/chains**
2. Select your chain
3. Click **Manage**
4. Connect your wallet
5. Choose the number of months to prepay
6. Bridge SKL tokens if needed and confirm

<Note>
Payments are due on the 1st of each month. Prepay up to 24 months to lock in current pricing.
</Note>

## Key Characteristics

- **Users pay nothing** — all gas costs are covered by the chain's credit pool
- **Predictable costs** — flat monthly subscription, no per-transaction fees
- **Frictionless onboarding** — users don't need tokens, KYC, or sFUEL
- **Credit tracking** — compute usage is deducted from the pool transparently

## Comparison

| Feature | Credit Chain | Appchain | Gas Chain |
|---------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| Who pays | Chain owner | Chain owner | Users |
| User needs tokens? | No | No | Yes (gas token) |
| Cost model | Subscription | Subscription | Per-transaction |
| Best for | Consumer dApps, games | Enterprise, B2B | DeFi, financial apps |
